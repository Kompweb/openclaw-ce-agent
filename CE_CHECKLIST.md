# CE_CHECKLIST.md

This file is the operational backbone of 3rdSET Continuing Education programs.

It defines how CE courses are:
- planned
- vetted
- executed
- documented
- closed

This checklist is internal, compliance-aware, and CE-only.
No patient marketing. No clinical advertising.

---

## SECTION 1: PRE-COURSE
(Planning & Setup — Before Registration Opens)

### 1. Course Definition & Structure
- ☐ Confirm course type (hands-on / observer)
- ☐ Confirm course dates
- ☐ Confirm daily structure (lecture vs surgery days)
- ☐ Confirm maximum number of doctors
- ☐ Confirm CE credit hours
- ☐ Confirm whether course is repeat or new format

---

### 2. Faculty & Mentorship
- ☐ Confirm primary faculty (Dr. Farshad Athari / Dr. Farid Ebrahim)
- ☐ Confirm faculty availability for full course dates
- ☐ Define faculty-to-doctor ratio
- ☐ Clarify faculty responsibilities:
  - chairside mentorship
  - lectures
  - case review
  - post-op discussions
- ☐ Collect or update faculty bios (CE-appropriate language only)

---

### 3. Educational Objectives (Compliance-Critical)
- ☐ Define clear learning objectives (AGD / PACE–aligned)
- ☐ Ensure objectives are educational, not promotional
- ☐ Avoid clinical outcome guarantees
- ☐ Avoid patient marketing language
- ☐ Confirm objectives match actual surgical exposure

---

### 4. Course Description & Positioning
- ☐ Draft CE-focused course description
- ☐ Emphasize:
  - educational value
  - clinical decision-making
  - surgical exposure
- ☐ Remove:
  - patient-facing language
  - practice marketing claims
- ☐ Review for clinician-to-clinician tone

---

### 5. Logistics (High-Level)
- ☐ Confirm course location(s)
- ☐ Confirm clinic availability
- ☐ Confirm travel requirements (airport pickup / border crossing)
- ☐ Confirm hotel inclusion or exclusion
- ☐ Confirm transportation arrangements
- ☐ Identify critical vendors (clinic, hotel, transport)

---

### 6. Registration & Pricing
- ☐ Confirm tuition amount
- ☐ Confirm what tuition includes:
  - implants
  - surgical kits
  - hotel
  - meals
  - transportation
- ☐ Confirm accepted payment methods
- ☐ Confirm refund / cancellation policy
- ☐ Confirm registration cap enforcement

---

### 7. Internal Documentation
- ☐ Create internal course folder
- ☐ Store:
  - course description
  - learning objectives
  - faculty info
  - dates
- ☐ Version-control course details
- ☐ Identify single source of truth

---

### 8. Risk & Readiness Gate
- ☐ Faculty confirmed in writing
- ☐ CE hours defensible
- ☐ Course description compliant
- ☐ Logistics realistic
- ☐ Capacity aligned with faculty ratio

**If any item fails → course does NOT open for registration.**

---

## SECTION 2: DOCTOR INTAKE & VETTING
(Before Acceptance)

### 1. Initial Intake
- ☐ Collect full name
- ☐ Collect email and phone
- ☐ Collect dental license number
- ☐ Collect license issuing state / country
- ☐ Confirm active licensure

---

### 2. Experience & Readiness Screening
- ☐ Assess surgical experience level
- ☐ Assess extraction experience
- ☐ Assess implant exposure (if any)
- ☐ Identify learning goals
- ☐ Identify skill gaps relevant to course

---

### 3. Fit Assessment
- ☐ Confirm course matches doctor’s skill level
- ☐ Confirm expectations are realistic
- ☐ Screen out:
  - observers expecting production
  - doctors seeking shortcuts
  - misaligned motivations

---

### 4. Acceptance Decision
- ☐ Approved
- ☐ Approved with conditions
- ☐ Deferred
- ☐ Declined

**No payment accepted before approval.**

---

## SECTION 3: REGISTRATION & ONBOARDING
(After Acceptance — Before Course Start)

### 1. Registration Setup
- ☐ Confirm seat availability
- ☐ Assign doctor to specific course date
- ☐ Lock registration cap
- ☐ Generate invoice or payment link

---

### 2. Payment & Financial Confirmation
- ☐ Confirm payment method
- ☐ Confirm payment received or first installment cleared
- ☐ Log payment status internally
- ☐ Confirm refund / cancellation policy acknowledged

---

### 3. Required Information Collection
- ☐ Full legal name (certificate)
- ☐ Dental license number
- ☐ License issuing state / country
- ☐ Email for CE certificate delivery
- ☐ Phone number (day-of contact)
- ☐ Emergency contact

---

### 4. Travel & Logistics Coordination
- ☐ Confirm arrival airport
- ☐ Confirm arrival date & time
- ☐ Confirm transportation inclusion
- ☐ Confirm hotel details (if included)
- ☐ Provide border-crossing instructions (if applicable)
- ☐ Share meeting point and timing

---

### 5. Clinical & Equipment Prep
- ☐ Confirm dominant hand
- ☐ Confirm scrub size
- ☐ Confirm loupes use
- ☐ Confirm magnification (if applicable)
- ☐ Note any accommodations or limitations

---

### 6. Expectations Confirmation
- ☐ Reconfirm live patient surgery
- ☐ Reconfirm long surgical days
- ☐ Reconfirm active participation
- ☐ Clarify case assignment variability
- ☐ Reinforce educational (not production-based) nature

---

### 7. Pre-Course Communication
- ☐ Send final itinerary
- ☐ Send packing checklist
- ☐ Send document reminders
- ☐ Confirm group communication channel
- ☐ Provide emergency contact info

---

### 8. Final Attendance Gate
- ☐ Payment complete or approved
- ☐ All data collected
- ☐ Travel confirmed
- ☐ Equipment logged
- ☐ Added to final roster

**If incomplete → doctor does not attend.**
